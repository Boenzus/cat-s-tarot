The Sacred Ritual

A beginner's path to the three-card spread

01

Set the Sanctuary

Find a quiet, secluded area where your spirit feels at peace. You may light a candle or incense to cleanse the space and sharpen your focus.

02

Focus the Intention

Ask a simple, open question. Do not seek a biased answer; instead, ask "What can I learn from this moment?" and hold this thought while shuffling.

03

Trinity Reveal

Divide the deck into three stacks. Open the top card of each. From left to right, they represent your **Past**, **Present**, and **Future Outcome**.

History of the Card

Pamela Colman Smith

"Pixie" • 1878 – 1951

This collection is an eternal tribute to the visionary work of Pamela Colman Smith. A British artist and occultist, her contributions redefined the language of the Tarot for the modern age.

In 1909, she achieved a breakthrough: she was the first to illustrate the Minor Arcana with full, scenic human imagery, transforming simple symbols into deep psychological stories.
