History of Card

Pamela Colman Smith

"Pixie" • 1878 – 1951

This collection is an eternal tribute to the visionary work of Pamela Colman Smith. A British artist, occultist, and author, her artistic contributions redefined the language of the Tarot for the modern age.

In 1909, she achieved a breakthrough of unprecedented historical significance: she was the first to illustrate the Minor Arcana with full, scenic human imagery, transforming what were once simple symbols into deep psychological stories.

Though her original art focused on the human soul, her "Pixie" spirit lives on in every interpretation that follows her structure. Cat Prophet celebrates her original archetypes by re-imagining them through this dedicated cat-themed creative lens.
